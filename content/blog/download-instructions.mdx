# How to Download and Install iTracksy

iTracksy is currently available for Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge. Follow the instructions below for your preferred browser to start using iTracksy today!

## Google Chrome

1. Visit the [iTracksy page on the Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/bubbybeep-your-ai-explora/cbibgdcbkoikdkeahemkjkpacmgicaco).
2. Click the "Add to Chrome" button.
3. A pop-up will appear asking for permissions. Click "Add extension" to proceed.
4. iTracksy will be downloaded and installed automatically.
5. Look for the iTracksy icon in your browser's toolbar to start using the extension.

## Microsoft Edge

1. Go to the [iTracksy page on the Microsoft Edge Add-ons store](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/okjbfkonbgmfhcekhdpbodcfadofdefg).
2. Click the "Get" button.
3. A pop-up will appear asking for permissions. Click "Add extension" to continue.
4. iTracksy will be downloaded and installed automatically.
5. Look for the iTracksy icon in your browser's toolbar to begin using the extension.

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during installation:

1. Make sure your browser is up to date.
2. Try disabling other extensions temporarily to check for conflicts.
3. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then attempt the installation again.
4. If problems persist, please contact our support team at support@itracksy.com.

## After Installation

Once you've successfully installed iTracksy:

1. Click on the iTracksy icon in your browser toolbar to open the extension.
2. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your account or log in.
3. Take a moment to customize your settings for the best experience.

We hope you enjoy using iTracksy to enhance your web browsing! If you have any questions or feedback, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team.

Happy browsing!
