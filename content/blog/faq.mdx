# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is iTracksy?

iTracksy is an AI-powered browser extension designed to enhance your web browsing experience. It allows you to capture, summarize, and interact with web content using advanced AI technology.

### Which browsers support iTracksy?

Currently, iTracksy is available for [list supported browsers, e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari]. We're working on expanding to other browsers in the future.

### Is iTracksy free?

iTracksy offers a free version with basic features. We also have a premium version with advanced capabilities. Visit our [pricing page](/pricing) for more details.

## Features

### How does the AI summarization work?

Our AI summarization feature uses advanced natural language processing to analyze web content and generate concise, accurate summaries. It's designed to capture the key points of an article or webpage quickly.

### Can I ask questions about the content I've saved?

Yes! Our interactive Q&A feature allows you to ask questions about any content you've captured. The AI will analyze the content and provide relevant answers based on the information available.

### How do I organize my saved content?

iTracksy allows you to tag and categorize your saved content for easy retrieval. You can also create custom folders to organize your captures according to your preferences.

## Privacy and Security

### Is my data safe with iTracksy?

We take your privacy and security seriously. All content processing is done securely, and we do not store your personal browsing history. For more details, please review our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).

### Can iTracksy access all my browsing data?

No, iTracksy only accesses the specific web pages you choose to capture or analyze. It does not have access to your overall browsing history or personal data.

## Technical Support

### How do I install iTracksy?

Visit our [Download Instructions](/blog/download-instructions) page for step-by-step guidance on installing iTracksy for your specific browser.

### I'm experiencing issues with iTracksy. What should I do?

First, try refreshing your browser or reinstalling the extension. If problems persist, please contact our support team at support@itracksy.com for assistance.

### How often is iTracksy updated?

We regularly update iTracksy to improve performance, add new features, and fix any issues. Your extension should update automatically, but you can always check for updates in your browser's extension settings.

## Getting Involved

### Can I contribute to iTracksy's development?

Absolutely! iTracksy is an open-source project, and we welcome contributions from the community. Visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/iTracksy/itracksy.com) to learn how you can get involved.

### Where can I suggest new features or report bugs?

We love hearing from our users! You can suggest features or report bugs by emailing our support team at support@itracksy.com or by creating an issue on our [GitHub issues page](https://github.com/iTracksy/itracksy.com/issues).

If you have any questions not covered here, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team. We're here to help!
